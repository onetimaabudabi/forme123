# Social Network

## ⭐ Stories

Users can publish temporary stories.

Features:

- Images
- Videos
- Text
- Music (future)

Stories disappear after 24 hours.

Users can react to stories.

Future:

- Story likes
- Story replies
- Story highlights

---

## ⭐ Saved Posts

Users can save posts.

Saved posts remain private.

Posts are synchronized between devices.

---

## ⭐ Post Drafts

Users can save unfinished posts.

Drafts include:

- text
- photos
- videos

---

## 🔥 Suggested Users

Recommend users based on:

- friends
- followers
- activity
- interests

---

## 🔥 Trending Feed

Display trending posts based on:

- likes
- comments
- engagement

Should update automatically.

---

## 🔥 Search Improvements

# Long-Term Vision

Forme should become more than a workout tracker.

It should become an intelligent fitness ecosystem where users can:

- Train
- Eat
- Track health
- Build friendships
- Complete challenges
- Customize avatars
- Learn through AI
- Stay motivated every day

The goal is to make Forme the application users open every morning and every evening as part of their healthy lifestyle.
